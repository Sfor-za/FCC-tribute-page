Max Planck

The Father of Quantum Mechanics

Here's a timeline of Plancks Life

  • 1858 : Planck was born in Kiel, Holstein
  • 1867 : His family moved to Munich, and Planck enrolled in the Maximilians gymnasium school
  • 1874 : Began his studies at the University of Munich. Under Philipp von Jolly's supervision
  • 1877 : He went to the Friedrich Wilhelms University in Berlin with Hermann von Helmholtz and Gustav Kirchhoff and Karl Weierstrass
  • 1878 : Planck passed his qualifying exam
  • 1879 : He defended his dissertation, Über den zweiten Hauptsatz der mechanischen Wärmetheorie.(On the second law of thermodynamics)
  • 1880 : Planck had obtained the two highest academic degrees offered in Europe
  • 1885 : The University of Kiel appointed Planck as associate professor of theoretical physics
  • 1889 : He was named the successor to Kirchhoff's position at the Friedrich-Wilhelms-Universität in Berlin
  • 1892 : Became a full professor
  • 1897 : He published his Treatise on Thermodynamics
  • 1900 : The first version of the famous Planck black-body radiation law was first proposed in a meeting of the DPG on 19 October
  • 1900 : He made the supposition, now known as the Planck postulate, that electromagnetic energy could be emitted only in quantized form, in other words, the energy could only be a multiple of an elementary unit: E=hv
  • 1907 : Planck was offered Boltzmann's position in Vienna, but turned it down to stay in Berlin
  • 1909 : As a University of Berlin professor, he was invited to become the Ernest Kempton Adams Lecturer in Theoretical Physics at Columbia University in New York City
  • 1918 : He was awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics
  • 1920s : Bohr, Heisenberg and Pauli had worked out the Copenhagen interpretation of quantum mechanics, but it was rejected by Planck, and by Schrödinger, Laue, and Einstein as well
  • 1926 : He retired from Berlin on 10 January and was succeeded by Erwin Schrödinger
  • 1944 : Planck's son Erwin was arrested by the Gestapo following the attempted assassination of Hitler in the 20 July plot
  • 1945 : Erwin was hanged at Berlin's Plötzensee Prison in January
  • 1947 : After the war had ended, Planck, his second wife, and their son were brought to a relative in Göttingen, where Planck died on October 4th
